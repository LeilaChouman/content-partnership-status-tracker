# Privacy Policy - Secure Team Tracker **Last Updated: October 12, 2025** ## Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how Secure Team Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our purchase order and campaign tracking application. ## Information We Collect ### Authentication Information - Email address (required for account creation and login) - Password (encrypted and securely stored via Firebase Authentication) - Email verification status ### User Activity Data - Purchase Order (PO) details including PO numbers, names, amounts, and expiry dates - Campaign information including names, suppliers, costs, and status updates - Invoice and Certificate of Completion (COC) tracking data - Project status and timeline information - Geographic region (GEO) selections ### Audit Logs - User login and logout timestamps - Actions performed within the application (create, update, delete operations) - User email associated with each action - Timestamps of all modifications ### Session Data - Active session duration - User interaction events (for session timeout management) ## How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: - Authenticate users and maintain secure access to the application - Enable real-time collaboration among team members - Track and manage purchase orders, campaigns, and invoicing workflows - Maintain audit trails for compliance and security purposes - Provide data export functionality (CSV downloads) - Manage session timeouts for enhanced security - Enable undo functionality for accidental deletions or modifications ## Data Storage and Security ### Security Measures - All data is stored in Firebase Firestore with encryption at rest - Passwords are encrypted using Firebase Authentication security protocols - Email verification is required before account activation - Automatic session timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity - All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL ### Data Location Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers. Firebase complies with industry-standard security practices and regulations. ## Data Sharing and Disclosure ### We DO NOT: - Sell your personal information to third parties - Share your data with advertisers - Use your data for marketing purposes - Transfer data outside the application's operational requirements ### We MAY share data: - With team members within your organization who have authenticated access - When required by law or legal process - To protect the security and integrity of our application - With Firebase/Google services that power our infrastructure ## User Rights You have the right to: - Access your personal information stored in the application - Request deletion of your account and associated data - Export your data in CSV format - Update or correct your information at any time - Revoke access by signing out and discontinuing use ## Data Retention - Active user data is retained as long as your account remains active - Audit logs are retained for compliance and security purposes - Deleted items can be restored via the undo function (limited to last 10 actions) - Upon account deletion request, all personal data will be removed within 30 days ## Cookies and Tracking The application uses: - Firebase session cookies for authentication - Local browser storage for session management - No third-party tracking or analytics cookies ## Email Verification We require email verification to: - Confirm account ownership - Prevent unauthorized access - Enable account recovery - Ensure communication channel validity ## Children's Privacy This application is intended for business use only and is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. ## Contact Information For privacy-related questions, data access requests, or account deletion: **Email:** admin@omc.com ## Compliance This application strives to comply with: - General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - Industry-standard data protection practices ## Your Consent By using Secure Team Tracker, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. --- **Note:** This is a basic privacy policy template. For production use, please consult with legal counsel to ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
